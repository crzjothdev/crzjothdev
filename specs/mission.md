# Mission

## Purpose

**crzjothdev** is Angelo Cruz's professional profile website — a living document of his identity as a software developer. It serves as the primary digital point of contact for anyone evaluating his technical capabilities, professional history, and personal projects.

## What This Site Achieves

This site exists to:

- **Demonstrate real-world competency** by showcasing completed projects, work history, and the breadth of technologies Angelo has worked with across frontend, backend, and DevOps domains.
- **Signal professional availability** openly and directly — making it easy for the right person to reach out whether for a full-time role or a consulting engagement.
- **Reflect continuous growth** by being a project that itself evolves with modern practices (the site is built with, and upgraded using, the same tools Angelo works with professionally).
- **Build personal brand** as `crzjothdev` — a recognisable identity in the developer community that connects his GitHub activity, personal projects, and professional presence.

## Target Audience

| Audience | What they are looking for |
|---|---|
| Recruiters & HR teams | Verified work history, skills overview, easy contact path |
| Potential clients | Project portfolio, consulting credibility, professional tone |
| Fellow developers | Code quality signal (site is open-source), tech choices, project ideas |

## Geographic Scope

Primarily targeting **Ireland and the global remote market**. The site must project a professional standard consistent with competitive international job markets — polished, fast, and accessible.

## Success Criteria

The site is doing its job when:

1. A recruiter or client can find the core information (who Angelo is, what he has built, how to contact him) in under 60 seconds.
2. The contact section generates at least one meaningful inbound conversation per month.
3. The codebase itself can be used as a reference project that demonstrates good engineering practices.
4. The site scores 90+ on all Lighthouse categories (Performance, Accessibility, Best Practices, SEO).
